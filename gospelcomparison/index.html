How to Use This Site

This site allows you to explore the parallels between the Gospels. You can use the verse finder at the top of each column to quickly navigate to any verse. The colors indicate how many parallel verses exist across the Gospels:

  • One parallel verse
  • Two parallel verses
  • Three or more parallel verses
  • Unique verse (no parallels)

Clicking on a verse will highlight it and its parallels in the other Gospels. The current chapter will always be visible at the top of the screen as you scroll through the text.
