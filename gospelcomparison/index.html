How to use this site:

This site allows you to explore the parallels between the Gospels. Click a verse and if it has parallels, the site will scroll to them and highlight them. Use the verse finder at the top of each column to jump to any verse. Colors indicate how many parallel verses exist across the Gospels (See legend on the right).
